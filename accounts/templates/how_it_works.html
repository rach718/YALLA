  1. CREATE AN EVENT (choose 2 potential dates and times, and 3 locations)

  3. INVITEES VOTE ANONYMOUSLY ON THEIR TOP CHOICE
  4. POPULAR VOTE WINS!
  5. INVITEES OFFICIALLY RSVP
  6. YAY! YOUR EVENT IS FINALIZED!
